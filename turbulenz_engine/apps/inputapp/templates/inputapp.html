When the mouse is over the TurbulenzEngine, the background is red, it is gray otherwise. A black border appears when the TurbulenzEngine has focus.

Clicking the "Lock" button, locks the mouse. To unlock the mouse, press the "Unlock" button or the Escape key.

Pad support is only available on Windows, and only for the Xbox 360 gamepad.

To see output logs, please activate the console (using Firebug or the built-in browser development tools).
